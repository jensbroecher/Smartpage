smartinfopage makes it easy to share your contact information and social media links with friends, business partners and customers. Create your own smartinfopage now, it's free and simple to get started!

Description

Sharing all our contact information gets more and more difficult, as we use a myriad of services. Phone, Fax, E-Mail, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn etc... Sharing all of this information via business cards is difficult and cumbersome. Your customers and friends are mobile (400% growth since 2010!) and you should be too! smartinfopage is like a digital extension of your businss card. With just a few easy steps, you can be connected to the world. Create your mobile friendly smartinfopage today that adapts perfectly to smartphone screens! smartinfopage gives you innovative technology to reach more people / customers each day.

Features

  • Create your tailored digital businss card / mobile website.
  • Link to all of your Social Media Accounts: Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube, Soundcloud etc...
  • Choose from a variety of cool designs to make your page stand out.
  • One touch dialing of your mobile, text etc.
  • GPS enabled Google Maps makes it easy to find you.
  • Upload your own images and logo.
  • Get your own QR-Code to share on business cards, posters, banners etc...
  • and much more...
